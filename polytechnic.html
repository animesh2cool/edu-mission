Dilpoma Engineering/ Ploytechnic

Polytechnic or Diploma Engineering courses are designed to provide practical and industry-oriented technical education. These programs are typically offered by polytechnic colleges and institutes across the state, aiming to produce skilled technicians and engineers who can contribute effectively to various industrial sectors.

Program Overview

  • Duration:  3 years (6 semesters)

                      2 years (Lateral Entry)

  • Medium of Instruction: Primarily English, though some institutes may offer courses in regional languages(Bengali/Hindi).
  • Affiliation: Courses are affiliated with the West Bengal State Council of Technical and Vocational Education and Skill Development (WBSCT&VE&SD) and are recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

Admission Requirements

  • Eligibility:
    • For 3 Years: 10th with minimum 35% Marks Required.
    • For 2 Years: 10+2 with Vocational(WBSCTE and VE) or must have PCM.

Key Specializations

Polytechnic courses in West Bengal offer various specializations catering to different engineering and technical fields. Key specializations include:

  • Civil Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering
  • Computer Science and Technology
  • Automobile Engineering
  • Information Technology
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Food Technology

Curriculum

The diploma engineering curriculum is designed to provide a balance between theoretical knowledge and practical skills. Key components include:

  • Core Subjects: Fundamental courses related to the chosen engineering discipline.
  • Practical Training: Extensive laboratory sessions and workshops to gain hands-on experience.
  • Project Work: Minor and major projects to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world problems.
  • Industrial Training: Internships and industry visits to understand industrial processes and practices.

Practical Training

  • Laboratory Work: Practical sessions in various engineering labs to develop technical skills.
  • Workshops: Hands-on training in workshops to work with tools and machinery.
  • Internships: Industrial training and internships to provide real-world exposure and experience.

Career Prospects

Graduates of Polytechnic or Diploma Engineering programs have diverse career opportunities in both public and private sectors:

  • Government Jobs: Roles in various state and central government departments, public sector undertakings (PSUs), and municipalities.
  • Private Sector: Opportunities in manufacturing industries, IT companies, construction firms, telecommunications, and more.
  • Higher Education: Lateral entry into the second year of B.Tech/B.E programs, pursuing AMIE (Associate Member of the Institution of Engineers) certification, or other advanced technical courses.
  • Entrepreneurship: Starting own ventures in technical services, small-scale industries, and consultancy services.

Course Fees :

  • For 3 Years: Starting from 1.05L
  • For 2 Years: Starting from 70K
