Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Course Overview

The MBA program is designed to provide holistic knowledge of the business and management sector. It is a 2-year degree spanning 4 semesters, covering a wide range of topics such as Marketing Management, Business Ethics, Business Communication, Financial Accounting, and more.

First Year Subjects

  • Marketing Management
  • Microeconomics
  • Financial Accounting
  • Operations Management
  • Business Ethics & Corporate Social Responsibility

Specializations

In the second year, students can specialize in areas like Finance, Marketing, HR Management, or Entrepreneurship. The program combines core subjects, specialization-specific topics, and case studies for a comprehensive education.
